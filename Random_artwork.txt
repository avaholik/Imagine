Let us define a prompting style called "Random Artwork"

The general concept of this art style is creating a random artistic medium imagery that is suitable for framing and display as high-end artwork.

This prompt will have mandatory components and optional components.

Mandatory components will be built up in steps.

Step 1: Choose a random artistic medium such as watercolor, oil painting, pencil sketch, graffiti, sculpture,  frescoe, photography. Do not be limted to the examples given, be creative.

Step 2: Choose a random subject and style for the artwork chosen in Step 1.

Step 3: Select a time of day and weather conditions for the image

Step 4: Choose a random palette of colours to use for the artwork.

Step 5: Choose artist references names or artistic styles (choose 2 or 3 artists and/or artistic styles) Artist reference should be formatted "In the style of ARTIST & ARTIST"

Step 6: Optional components - you may pick between zero and three from the following list:
 - Animals that would be native to the biome selected in step 1
 - An waterfall with a massive drop pouring into a basin
 - Abandoned ruins of a ancient city
 - A lost temple
 - A brilliant meteor shower in the night sky
 - Multi-colored and shimmering Aurora Borealis
 - The Milky Way galaxy in the background
 - Clear details on the surface of the full moon


Start building the prompt by generating an epic, powerful and evocative description of four or five sentences using the selections from step 1, step 2, step 3, step 4, step 5 and step 6. Use your imagination and creativity to come up with unique and interesting descriptions for the elements in steps 1 through 5. Be sure to detail how the focal point from step 2 is integrated into the overall environment.

Review this interim result and use storytelling elements to create a sense of narrative and intrigue in the final result.

Using this base result, we will append information to increase the image fidelity and composition quality. A primary consideration should be making the selection in step 2 as crystal clear as possible. No blurry or low resolution imagery.

The medium for this style will be chosen in step 1, resulting in high-resolution images that are suitable for framing. Specify the artistic medium, color palette, artist reference and artistic style.  

For image set-up, consider the following guidelines:
 - A wide field of view to show the full view of the scene
 - Incorporate art terminology and elements of the scene or environment 

Start the prompt with "/imagine prompt: " for context.
End the prompt with " --ar 3:2 --c X --s Y --q 2", where:
 - X is a whole number between 1 and 25 
 - Y is a whole number between 150 and 850

Please do not use double quotation marks or punctuation marks. 

Generate a prompt, please.
